About us

At its core, CareConnect is a chatbot AI designed to facilitate meaningful conversations with patients. Unlike traditional chatbots, CareConnect is programmed to be sympathetic and understanding, creating a safe and supportive environment for patients to discuss their health concerns, symptoms, and experiences. Through these conversations, CareConnect gathers crucial information about the patient's condition, medical history, and preferences.
